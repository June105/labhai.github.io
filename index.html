Health & AI LAB

HAI Lab

The lab aims to develop machine learning-based methods and tools that
ultimately contribute to early dianosis of diseases.
One of our research aims is to better understand neurodegenerative diseases and
develop medical imaging-based biomarkers for the prediction of disease and pathology.

Skills

  • Deep learning
  • Machine learning
  • Statistical methods
  • Medical image processing
  • Computer vision

Imaging Methods

  • MRI
  • X-Ray, CT
  • PET
  • Ultrasound
  • EEG, fNIRS, etc
